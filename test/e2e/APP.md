# FitTrack

**Tagline:** Your personal fitness companion.

FitTrack is a simple, beautiful fitness tracking app that helps users build healthy habits through daily step counting, workout logging, and goal setting.

## Target Audience

Health-conscious adults aged 25-45 who want a no-nonsense fitness tracker without the complexity of full gym apps. Available worldwide with localization for English, French, and Arabic markets.

## Key Features

1. **Step Tracking** — Automatic daily step counting with progress rings and milestone celebrations.
2. **Workout Logging** — Quick-add workouts with type, duration, and intensity. Supports running, cycling, swimming, yoga, and strength training.
3. **Smart Goals** — Set weekly targets and get nudged when you're falling behind.
4. **Progress Insights** — Weekly and monthly charts showing trends, streaks, and personal bests.
5. **Social Proof** — Over 50,000 active users. 4.8-star rating on the App Store.
